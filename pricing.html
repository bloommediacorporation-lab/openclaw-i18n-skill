Billing FAQ

How does billing work? +
We bill monthly through Lemon Squeezy. Your first 7 days are free, then €5/month starting day 8.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes! Cancel anytime from your Lemon Squeezy dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What happens after the free trial? +
Nothing automatic. We'll remind you before day 7, and you can upgrade if you want to continue. No credit card required to start.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. Contact us within 14 days of payment for a full refund.

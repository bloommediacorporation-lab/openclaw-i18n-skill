The Problem

❌

Broken Diacritics

Your AI outputs " Romania" instead of "România", "munca" instead of "muncă" — errors that make your brand look amateur.

❌

Stray Characters

Random Chinese or Cyrillic characters appear mid-sentence: "Bună理论 ziua" — confusing users and breaking trust.

❌

Wrong Locale Formatting

Dates show as MM/DD/YYYY instead of localized formats. Currency symbols in wrong positions. Phone numbers broken.

The Solution

Two layers of protection against broken non-English output.

📋 SKILL.md Layer

Configures your agent to think in the correct language. Auto-detection, locale-aware formatting, and cultural communication norms baked in.

⚙️ Post-Processor

A Python engine that mechanically cleans every response before it reaches the user. Fixes diacritics, removes artifacts, normalizes whitespace.

🎯 High Accuracy

Conservative rules with 95%+ confidence. We never guess — when uncertain, we leave text unchanged. No false positives.
